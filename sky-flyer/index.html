SKY DRIFT 🌙

Moonlit mountains, a dreaming forest, and nowhere to be.
🏮 Somewhere below, someone is wishing on a lantern.
Every lantern light you carry to the great glowing tree completes one wish.
You are a Wanderer — fly with your friends, and complete the wishes of the world ✨
And when your wings tire — chairs wait on the summits, five of them around one fire 🔥
